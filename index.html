Evers vetoes bill opting Wisconsin into federal voucher expansion: Gov. Tony Evers rejected Assembly Bill 602, which would have required Wisconsin to join the nationwide private school voucher expansion tied to the federal proposal. Evers cited a lack of accountability, including no student performance metrics or spending limits, and warned the program could divert significant funding away from public schools.
